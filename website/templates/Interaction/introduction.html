Explanation of the Interaction Procedure

The dialogue is divided into 3 parts. Each part focuses on one specific question and lasts 5 minutes.

Your task during each 5-minute section is to understand your conversation partner’s views on the given question as accurately as possible. Your partner has the same task. After the conversation, you will be asked questions to assess how well you understood your partner’s viewpoint.

Please try to start the interaction punctually at the scheduled time.

Rules for the Dialogue

1. Treat your conversation partner with respect.

2. Be polite when interacting with your conversation partner.

3. Allow your conversation partner to speak and finish their thoughts.

4. Avoid judging your partner’s viewpoints.

5. Recording the video call is strictly prohibited.
