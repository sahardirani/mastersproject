Climate Activism & Climate Glue Protesters

Here you have the opportunity to participate in the political discourse on "climate glue protesters." This term refers to activists from the group "The Last Generation," who aim to draw attention to the threat of climate change through extreme protest actions.

Their most well-known protest method involves gluing themselves to streets, thereby blocking traffic. The group also engages in actions such as throwing food at artworks or spray-painting private jets with graffiti.

These actions provoke very different and often highly emotional reactions among citizens. It is therefore important to engage in a factual discussion on this controversial topic.
