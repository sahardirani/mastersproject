Political Dialogue Platform


On this platform, participants can engage in respectful discussions on a variety of current and socially relevant topics. The aim is to enable constructive dialogue between individuals with different perspectives.

The platform is designed to cover a wide range of public issues, such as technological change, social values, human rights, economic developments, and other political questions that shape modern society. For the purpose of testing and evaluation, we currently focus on two example topics: artificial intelligence and its impact on employment, and freedom of speech on social media platforms.

These kinds of topics often generate strong and diverse reactions. It is therefore important to exchange views in a calm, fair, and constructive manner, allowing for deeper understanding between participants.
